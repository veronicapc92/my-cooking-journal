Mediterranean Salad

Let food be thy medicine, and let medicine be thy food.

â€”Hippocrates

Summer is here! And to cope with the heat, today I'm bringing you a perfectly balanced Mediterranean salad recipe. You can serve it as a side dish or make a meal out of it, this salad is healthy, refreshing and delicious! And it will only take you 15 min to prepare it.

Ingredients (2 people):

  • 250g tomatoes
  • 2 avocados
  • 250g lamb's lettuce
  • 100g feta cheese
  • 70g black olives
  • 3 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 70ml olive oil
  • A pinch of salt
    •

How to:

  1. Place the diced tomatoes, avocados and feta cheese along with the olives and lamb's lettuce in a bowl. Add salt and mix everything.
  2. Add the olive oil and the balsamic vinegar and mix everything one more time.
  3. Enjoy!
